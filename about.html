The Sousse Collegiate Programming Contest

The Sousse Collegiate Programming Contest (SoCPC) is a qualifying round for the ACM Tunisian Collegiate Programming Contest (TCPC). SoCPC is organized in the same spirit and is governed by all applicable rules of the ICPC. Winners of an SoCPC contest are qualified for TCPC. An SoCPC Regional Contest is normally held during Mars/April of each year, whereas the TCPC World Final is held in June/July of the following year.

The 2018 Second Sousse Collegiate Programming Contest (SoCPC 2018) will be held at The National Engineering School of Sousse from 7 to 8 of April, 2018.




First Edition

The first Sousse Collegiate Programming Contest was hosted by The National Engineering School of Sousse (ENISo) in 2017 thanks to the efforts of Mr. Taha Ben Salah,the director of Computing Engineering Department at the National School of Engineers of Sousse. Three Universities from Sousse participated in the first SoCPC.
